The origin story

From backpackers to local guides.

2016

Linh quits her bank job. Starts leading food tours for backpacker friends.

2018

Tuan joins after driving Linh through Ha Giang on a whim. The loop is born.

2020

Pandemic hits. We pivot to online cooking classes and stay connected.

2022

We open a Hanoi office. Still just a room above a bánh mì shop.

2026

2,400+ travelers later, we're still on plastic stools.
