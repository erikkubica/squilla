Hello Vietnam Adventures is a registered tour operator based in Hanoi, Vietnam (License #HN-2021-0847). We operate this website and handle bookings through it.

When you book a trip, fill out a form, or email us, we collect your name, email, phone number, dietary needs, and any other info you share. We also collect anonymous analytics (how many people visited the site, which pages are popular).

To run your trip, coordinate logistics, send booking confirmations, and occasionally email you about new experiences (only if you opt in). That's it.

4. Your rights

You can ask us to show you everything we have on file, correct it, or delete it. Just email xinchao@hellovietnam.co.
